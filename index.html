About RackHands

Chicago's trusted data center hands

Since 2017, RackHands has been the go-to smart hands team for companies operating in Chicago's colocation market. We provide skilled, badged technicians who are already authorized inside the city's key facilities — ready to execute physical tasks on your behalf without the delay of access requests or escort coordination.

As Chicago's data center market continues to expand, enterprises and cloud providers need reliable on-site presence without the overhead of a dedicated local team. That's exactly what we offer: professional hands, real accountability, and full documentation on every job.
